Russian Blue Cat

Tom from Tom and Jerry is thought to be a Russian Blue due to his bluish gray color and bright green eyes.

  • Scientific name: Felis catus
  • Other names: Archangel Blue, Archangel Cat
  • Origin: Arkhangelsk, Russia
  • Average lifespan: 15 to 20 years

Russian Blues are plush short-haired, shimmering pale blue-gray cats with emerald green eyes. This breed is long-lived thanks to general good health and a lack of genetic problems. Some Russians have even lived to 25 years. Curious and tranquil animal. They are known for their friendliness and intelligence.
